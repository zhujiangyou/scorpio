Tea break is a leisure time during work.It is arranged to provide some hot drinks (mainly coffee/tea) and some desserts, fruits, etc. during the rest of the work and meeting process, sometimes according to the requirements and seasons of the staff or meeting organizers. Change, add a portion of soft drinks (cola, juice, mineral water, etc.) and some sandwiches. In this moment, everyone gathers, drinks tea, and chats to soothe the mind and body.
